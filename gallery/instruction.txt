How to Play
1. Snakes
  I. Controls
     Use arrow keys (Up, Down, Left, Right) to control the snake's direction.
  II. Objective
     Eat the food items that appear on the screen to grow longer.
     Avoid collisions with the walls and the snake's own body.
  III. Scoring
     Each food item eaten increases the score.
     The longer the snake, the higher the score.
  IV. Game Over
     The game ends if the snake collides with the walls or itself.

2. Space Dodge
  I. Controls
     Use arrow keys or on-screen controls to maneuver the spaceship.
  II. Objective
     Dodge asteroids, or other obstacles.
     Survive as long as possible to achieve the highest score.
  III. Challenges
     Obstacles may appear at different speeds and trajectories, increasing difficulty.
  IV. Scoring
     Points are earned based on the time survived and obstacles avoided.
